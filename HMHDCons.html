HMHD Governing Equations

The HMHD model solves the following governing equations of ion mass density \(\rho\), magnetic field \(\mathbf{B}\), bulk velocity \(\mathbf{u}\), pressure \(p\), and electron pressure \(p_e\) [37, 9]:

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} + \nabla\!\cdot(\rho\,\mathbf{u}) &= 0 \\[6pt] \frac{\partial (\rho \mathbf{u})}{\partial t} + \nabla\!\cdot\!\left[\;\mathbf{u}\,\rho\,\mathbf{u} + \mathbf{I}\,p + \mathbf{I}\,p_e + \mathbf{I}\,\frac{B^2}{2\mu_0} - \frac{\mathbf{B}\mathbf{B}}{\mu_0}\right] &= \rho\,\mathbf{g} \\[6pt] \frac{\partial \varepsilon}{\partial t} + \nabla\!\cdot\!\left[ \mathbf{u}\!\left(\varepsilon + p - \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0}\right) + (\mathbf{u}+\mathbf{u}_H)\!\cdot\!\frac{\left(\mathbf{I}\,B^2 - \mathbf{B}\mathbf{B}\right)}{\mu_0} - \mathbf{B}\times\eta\,\mathbf{J} \right] &= 0 \\[6pt] \frac{\partial p_e}{\partial t} + \nabla\!\cdot(p_e\,\mathbf{u}_e) &= (1-\gamma)\,p_e\,\nabla\!\cdot\mathbf{u}_e \\[6pt] \frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t} &= -\,\nabla\times\mathbf{E} \end{aligned} \]

Definitions

\[ \begin{aligned} \varepsilon &= \frac{p}{\gamma-1} + \frac{\rho\,u^2}{2} + \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0},\\[4pt] n_e &= n = \frac{\rho}{m},\\[4pt] \mathbf{J} &= \frac{1}{\mu_0}\,\nabla\times\mathbf{B},\\[4pt] \mathbf{u}_e &= \mathbf{u} - \frac{\mathbf{J}}{e\,n_e}. \end{aligned} \]

Notation: \(\mathbf{I}\) is the identity tensor, \(B^2=\mathbf{B}\cdot\mathbf{B}\), \(\mu_0\) is the vacuum permeability, \(e\) is the elementary charge, and \(\gamma\) is the adiabatic index.
