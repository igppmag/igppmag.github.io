Religion: The Conservation Law

The Hall MHD model solves the following governing equations of ion mass density \(\rho\), magnetic field \(\mathbf{B}\), ion bulk velocity \(\mathbf{u}\), ion pressure \(p\), and electron pressure \(p_e\):

\[ \begin{gather} \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} + \nabla\!\cdot(\rho\,\mathbf{u}) = 0 \\[6pt] \rho\,\frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t} + \rho\,(\mathbf{u}\!\cdot\!\nabla)\mathbf{u} = -\nabla P - \nabla P_e + \mathbf{G} + \mathbf{J}\times\mathbf{B} \\[6pt] \frac{\partial P}{\partial t} + (\mathbf{u}\!\cdot\!\nabla)P + \gamma\,P\,\nabla\!\cdot\!\mathbf{u} = \mathbf{G}\!\cdot\!\mathbf{u} + \eta\,\mathbf{J}^{2} \\[6pt] \frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t} = -\,\nabla\times\mathbf{E} \\[6pt] \end{gather} \]

Instead of the original form shown above, the BATSRUS code actrually solves the conservative form of these equations, by default.

Definitions

\[ \begin{aligned} \mathbf{E} &= \eta\,\mathbf{J} -\,\mathbf{u}\times\mathbf{B} + \frac{\mathbf{J}\times\mathbf{B}}{e\,n_e} - \frac{\nabla P_e}{e\,n_e} \varepsilon &= \frac{p}{\gamma-1} + \frac{\rho\,u^2}{2} + \frac{B^2}{2\mu_0}, & n_e = n = \frac{\rho}{m},\\[4pt] \mathbf{J} &= \frac{1}{\mu_0}\,\nabla\times\mathbf{B}, & \mathbf{u}_e = \mathbf{u} - \frac{\mathbf{J}}{e\,n_e}. \end{aligned} \]

Notation: \(\mathbf{I}\) is the identity tensor, \(B^2=\mathbf{B}\cdot\mathbf{B}\), \(\mu_0\) is the vacuum permeability, \(e\) is the elementary charge, \(\eta\) is the resistivity, and \(\gamma\) is the adiabatic index.

Most ion inertial length \(d_i\) formulas are written in cgs, including wiki. I write it in SI here: \(\lambda_i=\sqrt{ \frac{m_i}{\mu_0 e^2 n_i} }\)[Toth et al. 2017].
